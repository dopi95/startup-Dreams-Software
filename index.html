About Us

the idea was born when Elyas Yenealem noticed that many local schools, hospitals and any other companies were still using manual systems to manage their daily tasks. As a passionate developer and student, i decided to create tools that solve real community problems through technology. I brought together a team of classmates and friends who believed in the power of local innovation. We are a group of 4 including me. There name is Bereket Eshete, Tenbite Daniel and Dagim Sisay. Together we launched Dreams Software to provide accessible, customized and practical software solution.

At Dreams Software, our mission is to empower local organizations by replacing outdated manual systems with smart, user-friendly digital tools. We strive to bridge the technology gap in Ethiopia by designing tailored software that enhances efficiency, improves decision-making, and creates meaningful impact for our clients. Through innovation and teamwork, we are committed to driving sustainable digital transformation from the grassroots level.

Dreams Software actively contributes to the local tech ecosystem by developing solutions that are not only functional but truly beneficial to underserved communities. From creating management systems for schools and clinics to offering affordable digital tools for small businesses, we focus on solving real problems that matter. We also mentor young students interested in tech, promote knowledge sharing, and encourage innovation as a pathway to social and economic growth.
